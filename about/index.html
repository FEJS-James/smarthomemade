About SmartHomeMade

We help you build a smarter home without the confusion.

What is SmartHomeMade?

SmartHomeMade is your no-BS guide to building a smart home. We write honest reviews, practical guides, and comparison articles to help you make smart decisions about smart home tech.

What We Cover

  • Product Reviews — We test smart home devices and tell you what's actually worth buying.
  • Buying Guides — Side-by-side comparisons so you don't have to research for hours.
  • How-To Guides — Step-by-step instructions for setting up and automating your home.
  • Smart Home Strategy — How to build a smart home without wasting money or creating a mess of incompatible devices.

Our Approach

We don't do sponsored reviews. We don't accept free products in exchange for coverage. Our recommendations are based on genuine testing and research. If something is good, we'll tell you. If it's not worth your money, we'll tell you that too.

Questions or suggestions? We'd love to hear from you.
